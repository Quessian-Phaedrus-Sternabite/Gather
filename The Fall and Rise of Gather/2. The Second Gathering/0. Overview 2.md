## Description
Gather has fallen. After [The Founder](0. Overview 1#^fa33b2) was brutally murdered by one of the members of Gather, though it is unknown who committed the grave crime. Tension amongst members of the group rose nigh instantly, with each deciding to use the death for the own ambitions. The Seven learn that the Founder left an unholy amount of money behind, with no easy way to access this.
